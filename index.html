Welcome to the Farm Oasis

Your Serene West Coast Retreat

Welcome to our serene oasis — beautiful farm views just steps from an amazing west coast beach. This brand-new suite is centrally located on the Saanich Peninsula, close to Victoria Airport (just 3 minutes away), the BC Ferry terminal, breathtaking hiking trails, shopping, and restaurants.

The suite features 2 spacious bedrooms each with full ensuite bathrooms, plus a loft with a queen bed and a powder bathroom in the main living & dining area. Everything is brand new — appliances, furniture, and modern finishes throughout.

Step outside your private entrance onto the patio with outdoor seating, surrounded by sweeping farm views. Watch bald eagles soar overhead, deer graze nearby, and blue herons stroll past — just steps from the water.

North Saanich, BC

Perfectly Situated

Located in beautiful North Saanich, our farm oasis puts you at the heart of everything the Saanich Peninsula has to offer. Just steps from gorgeous Pat Bay Beach, yet minutes from Victoria's most beloved attractions.

Whether you're catching a flight at Victoria International Airport (YYJ), exploring the world-famous Butchart Gardens, hopping on BC Ferries, or discovering charming Sidney — you're perfectly placed for all of it.

🦅 Wildlife right at your doorstep — Eagles, deer, ducks, blue herons, and an abundance of west coast wildlife regularly visit the property. A magical setting you simply won't find at any hotel.
